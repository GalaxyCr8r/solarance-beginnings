Spent a long weekend trying to figure out why my drafts kept drifting back toward EVE. The combat I'd cut kept finding its way back in. Mining minigames I'd parked kept getting added back to the next-up list.

So I wrote a player archetype — one specific composite player, with a name and an age and a Steam library. Every feature now passes a test: does this serve him?

Most of them don't. That's fine. The ones that do are the ones in the MVP.
